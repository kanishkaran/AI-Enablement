
EMPLOYEE HANDBOOK – HR GUIDELINES
Technology & Automation Organization

Welcome to the Company. This handbook explains everyday workplace rules and expectations.

WORKPLACE VALUES
We promote respect, collaboration, transparency, and continuous learning.

WORK HOURS
Employees generally work 8–9 hours per day. Teams may operate across time zones depending on client needs.

ATTENDANCE & COMMUNICATION
Employees should notify their team lead in case of late arrival, absence, or emergencies.

LEAVES
Available leaves include:
- Paid Time Off
- Sick Leave
- Compensatory Off
- Parental Leave

REMOTE WORK
Employees working from home must maintain confidentiality, secure devices, and uninterrupted connectivity.

CONDUCT AT WORK
Harassment, discrimination, or unethical behavior will not be tolerated.

USE OF COMPANY SYSTEMS
Company laptops, cloud accounts, and automation tools must be used responsibly.

FEEDBACK & CONCERNS
Employees may approach HR for feedback, concerns, or conflict resolution.

SEPARATION
Resignation must be submitted via official channels and approved by management.

This handbook is intended for guidance and does not constitute a contract.
